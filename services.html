Dog Walking

Your dog will be collected by me and transported in my van to a suitable area to walk, ranging from fields to woodland areas. Dogs will be walked in a group, free to sniff, roam and socialise together. If you would prefer your dog to be walked alone, just let me know and I will do my best to accommodate your needs. I will make sure dogs are kept hydrated, especially in the warmer weather.
From £10, additional dogs from the same household £7
Note to dog owners: Please let me know if your female dog isn't spayed and when she's due into season, as this may result in my group walk service not being available.

House sitting

House sitting is available for any animal, I will stay at your house while you're away and treat your pets as if they were my own, providing all the love and attention they need. This is very popular with dogs, rabbits and cats. Feel free contact me for more information on how I'd care for your pets.
From £30

Let outs and Home visits

Dogs: If you would rather your dog stayed at home while you're at work or out for the day, I can visit your dog instead. This ranges from a let out in the garden with a water bowl top up, to a longer visit including feeding and cuddles. Cats and small animals: While you're away I can visit your animals at home, twice a day if needed. This can include feeding, changing water, cuddle/play time and cleaning out cages if required. Please contact me for more information on the service provided.
From £7

Pet taxi

I am able to transport your pet somewhere if you're not able to. This includes vet visits and taking your pets to grooming appointments.
Prices depend on distance and availability.

Key Holding

If you are not home during the day, then please consider my complimentary safe key holding service, this is a lot safer than leaving a key out for me and is always handyincase you need a spare. This can be a huge benefit if you are ever running late or have an emergency, give me a call and I can check up on your pets for you.

Small animal boarding

While you're on holiday or away for the weekend, your small animals can come to stay with me. All small furries are welcome but will be to come with their cages, bedding, food ect as this will not be supplied.
From £10

A little bit about Sammie

I live in Chawton along with our elderly Labrador, two nutty Cocker Spaniels, six chickens, one pampered hamster and ten tone of fish. I've always had a passion for animals, especially dogs! Growing up, my family have owned a variety of pets, ranging from ferrets to rabbits. After leaving college with two diplomas in media, I decided it really wasn't for me, so I began searching for something new. After 8 years working at the local bistro, I finally decided to leave my job and combine my two passions, animals and the outdoors. Everyday I'm I'm extremely thankful I made the decision, and I'm glad to say I finally love what I'm doing.
