Dog Walking

Your dog will be collected by me and transported in my van to a suitable area to walk, ranging from fields to woodland areas. Your dog will be walked in a group with others, free to sniff, roam and socialise together. If you would prefer your dog to be walked alone, just let me know and I will do my best to accommodate your needs. I will make sure dogs are kept hydrated, especially in the warmer weather. From £10, additional dogs from the same household £7 Note to dog owners: Please let me know if your female dog isn't spayed and when she's due into season, as this may result in my group walk service not being available.

House sitting

House sitting is available for any animal If you don't feel comfortable leaving a pet alone at home while you're on holiday, or would rather your pet didn't have to leave it's home, this may be the best option. I will stay at your house while you're away and treat your pets as if they were my own, providing all the love and attention they need. This is very popular with dogs, rabbits and cats. Feel free contact me for more information or how I'd care for your pets. From £30

Let outs and Home visits

Dogs: If you would rather your dog stayed at home while you're at work or away, I can visit your dog instead. This ranges from a let out in the garden with a water bowl top up, to a longer visit including feeding and cuddles. This is particularly popular with puppies or older dogs that aren't able to take a trip out with me. Cats and small animals: While you're away I can visit your animals at home, twice a day if needed. This will include feeding, changing water, cuddle/play time and cleaning out cages if required. Please contact me for more information on the service provided. From £7

Pet taxi

I am able to transport your pet somewhere if you're not able to. This includes vet visits and taking your pets to grooming appointments. Prices depend on distance and availability.

Key Holding

If you are not home during the day, then please consider my complimentary safe key holding service. I can hold a set of keys for you which enables me to have access to your pets whenever needed, this is a lot safer than leaving a key out for me. This can be a huge benefit if you are ever running late or have an emergency, give me a call and I can check up on your pets for you. Also if you misplace or lose your own keys, just let me know and you can collect your keys from me (or if possible I can drop them off to you).

Medication

Please let me know if any of your animals need medication, when information and instructions are provided I am happy to administer it.
