Is your dog alone at home while you’re at work? Going on holiday and no one is able to care for your pet? Need a helping hand due to a busy schedule? Then Sammie's Ark may be your solution! Here at Sammie’s Ark I provide a loving and caring pet service. Animals in my care benefit from being looked after in a familiar surrounding, with a face they get to know and love. I offer a wide range of quality pet care services to fit around you.


A little bit about Sammie

I live in Chawton along with our elderly Labrador, two nutty Cocker Spaniels, six chickens, one pampered hamster and ten tone of fish. I've always had a passion for animals, especially dogs! Growing up, my family have owned a variety of pets, ranging from ferrets to rabbits. After leaving college with two diplomas in media, I decided it really wasn't for me, so I began searching for something new. After 8 years working at the local bistro, I finally decided to leave my job and combine my two passions, animals and the outdoors. Everyday I'm I'm extremely thankful I made the decision, and I'm glad to say I finally love what I'm doing.
